Instructions:
1. When you start, choose an available direction to go until you reach a door.
2. Answer the trivia question correctly. If it is correct, you can pass through. Otherwise, the door locks and you cannot pass through.
3. You win once you reach the exit.
4. You lose if you answer incorrectly too many times and run out of available paths. You can retry.
5. You can save, load, or exit out of your game any time.
